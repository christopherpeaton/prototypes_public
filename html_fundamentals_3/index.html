About Me

My name is Tanner Bierbaum. I am 22 years old, and currently reside in San Clemente, California. I grew up in here in San Clemente, and attended the local high school. I am a California State Lifeguard, and have been serving at the public beaches in San Clemente since 2009. I grew up in the water, joining both the water polo and swim team in high school, which motivated me to become a lifeguard. Upon graduation, I attended the University of Washington in Seattle, but later returned home after reconsidering what I wanted to study.I have spent the last six months exploring the world of programming and computer science, and am eager to achieve proficiency in web development.

Skills

  • Back-end Web Development: I want to see how our chosen back-end programming language interacts with the server to complete requests and demands coming from the front-end
  • Best Coding Practices: I want to learn what practices are considered most efficient and appropriate according to different circumstances, and how to excercise creativity in designing new layouts

Experience

As a lifeguard I learned to be observant. I learned to identify danger as it developed, keeping an eye out for trouble before it became present. The long hours in the tower test my endurance, and I grow weary watching the water and the crowds of people. But at the end of the day, I sleep deeply, knowing my efforts were reason for a day well spent.
