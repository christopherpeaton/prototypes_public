Instructions for Application - Academic Year 2014

  1. The deadline for 2014-2015 scholarship application: March 30,2014. Applications received after March 30 will be considered on a funds available basis
  2. Only completed applications will be considered. Do Not Leave Items Blank.
  3. Attach a current copy of your high school and/or college transcripts.
  4. Two signed letters of recommnedation (from non-relatives) must be attached to this application

Consideration for any scholarship award is based on a point system including GPA, participation in extra-curricular activites, school organization and community service. This will be a four semester scholarship, and you must maintain criteria set in the guidelines.
