Instructions for Application 2014

  1. The deadline for scholarship application: March 30, 2015. Applications received after this date will be will be considered on a funds available basis
  2. Only completed applications will be considered. Do Not Leave Items Blank.
  3. Attach a current copy of your high school and/or college transcripts.
  4. Two signed letters of recommendation (from non-relatives) must be attached to this application

Consideration for any scholarship award is based on a point system including GPA, participation in extra-curricular activities, school organization and community service. This will be a four semester scholarship, and you must maintain criteria set in the guidelines.
